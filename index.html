Problem Statement

Many individuals and families frequently waste food and money at home due to a lack of visibility and awareness of the items currently stored in their refrigerators.

Affinity Diagram

This affinity diagram breaks down the problem of food waste at home and solving it through fridge technology. It encompasses various aspects such as problem identification, potential product features, basic functionalities, cost considerations, constants, and advertisement strategies.

Sketches

Nibbles is a fridge management app designed to reduce waste and save money. Users can track contents of their fridge, see expiring foods, discover recipes, or donate excess food.
